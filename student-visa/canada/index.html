Canada Study Permit Requirements

Study at a Canadian institution. You need a letter of acceptance, proof of funds, and biometrics.

Home / Student Visa / Canada
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

A study permit lets you study at a designated learning institution in Canada. You need an acceptance letter, proof of funds, and usually biometrics. Apply online via IRCC.

Eligibility

  • Letter of acceptance from a DLI.
  • Proof of funds for tuition and living costs.
  • Valid passport; no criminal/medical inadmissibility.
  • Intent to leave Canada after studies.

Required Documents

  • Letter of acceptance from Canadian institution.
  • Proof of funds (bank statements, GIC, sponsorship).
  • Passport and photos; completed application form.
  • English/French test results if required.
  • Biometrics at a VAC when requested.

Application Process

  1. Get acceptance letter; create IRCC account and complete application.
  2. Upload documents and pay fees; give biometrics if required.
  3. Wait for decision; may need medical exam or more documents.
  4. If approved, get study permit; apply for visa/eTA if needed for entry.

Important Tips

  • Apply early; show strong funds and clear study plan.
  • Use a GIC if required for your country.
  • Keep documents consistent; explain gaps if any.
