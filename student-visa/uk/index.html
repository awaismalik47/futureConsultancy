United Kingdom

UK Student Visa Requirements

Study in the UK. You need a CAS from your university, proof of funds, and English language evidence.

Home / Student Visa / United Kingdom
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

UK Student visa requires a CAS from your university, proof of tuition and maintenance funds, and meeting the English requirement. You apply online and give biometrics at a VAC.

Eligibility

  • CAS from a licensed UK sponsor.
  • Funds for course fees and living costs (as per UKVI).
  • English at required level (SELT or degree in English).
  • TB test if from a listed country.

Required Documents

  • CAS reference number and documents listed on CAS.
  • Passport and photos; proof of funds (bank statements).
  • English language certificate (if required).
  • TB certificate if applicable.
  • ATAS clearance for certain courses (if required).

Application Process

  1. Get CAS from university; complete online application and pay fee + IHS.
  2. Submit documents and book VAC appointment for biometrics.
  3. Decision usually within a few weeks; may request more documents.
  4. If granted, collect passport with visa or receive by courier.

Important Tips

  • Funds must be held for 28 consecutive days; check exact amount on UKVI.
  • Apply before CAS expires; keep documents consistent with CAS.
  • Do not travel before the visa start date on your vignette.
