New Zealand Student Visa Requirements

Study in New Zealand. You need an offer of place, proof of funds, and meet health and character requirements.

Home / Student Visa / New Zealand
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

New Zealand Student visa requires an offer of place from an approved provider, proof of funds for fees and living costs, and meeting health and character requirements. Apply online via Immigration NZ.

Eligibility

  • Offer of place from approved NZ education provider.
  • Funds for tuition and living (as per INZ).
  • Health and character requirements.
  • English level if required by course.

Required Documents

  • Offer of place and proof of fee payment.
  • Passport and photos; proof of funds.
  • Health and character documents if required.
  • English test result (if required).
  • Evidence of onward travel or funds to leave.

Application Process

  1. Get offer of place; create Immigration NZ account and apply online.
  2. Upload documents and pay fee; may need medical or police certificate.
  3. Wait for decision; provide more information if requested.
  4. If granted, receive e-visa or notification; travel with proof of visa.

Processing Time & Fee

ItemDetails
Visa feeCheck immigration.govt.nz for current amount.
ProcessingVaries; typically several weeks.

Verify on the official Immigration New Zealand website.

Important Tips

  • Show clear funds; explain source if large deposits.
  • Provide health/character documents early if required.
  • Apply in good time before course start.
