United States

US Student Visa (F-1) Requirements

Study at a US institution. You need an I-20, SEVIS payment, and a visa interview.

Home / Student Visa / United States
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

F-1 is for full-time study at a US college or university. You must have an I-20 from your school, pay the SEVIS fee, and attend a visa interview with proof of funds and intent to return.

Eligibility

  • Acceptance at a US institution (I-20).
  • Sufficient funds for tuition and living costs.
  • Valid passport (6+ months).
  • Non-immigrant intent; ties to home country.

Required Documents

  • I-20 from the US school.
  • SEVIS fee payment receipt.
  • DS-160 confirmation and photo.
  • Proof of funds (bank statements, sponsorship).
  • Academic transcripts and English test (if required).

Application Process

  1. Get I-20 from your US school; pay SEVIS fee.
  2. Complete DS-160 and pay visa fee; book interview.
  3. Attend interview at US Embassy/Consulate with all documents.
  4. If approved, passport returned with F-1 visa.

Processing Time & Fee

ItemDetails
SEVIS + visa feeCheck current amounts on travel.state.gov
ProcessingFew weeks; interview wait times vary by location.

Verify fees and processing on the official US Department of State website.

Important Tips

  • Apply early; bring clear proof of funds and ties to home country.
  • Answer interview questions honestly and briefly.
  • Do not enter the US before the I-20 program start date.
