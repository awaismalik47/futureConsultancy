New Zealand Visitor Visa Requirements

Requirements for visiting New Zealand for tourism or business. Prepare your application with clear, professional guidance.

Home / Visit Visa / New Zealand
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

The New Zealand Visitor visa allows you to enter for tourism, family visits, or short business trips. Depending on your nationality, you may need a visa or a New Zealand Electronic Travel Authority (NZeTA). Applications are submitted online; you must show you are a genuine visitor and have sufficient funds and onward travel.

Eligibility

  • Purpose: Tourism, holiday, family visit, or business (e.g. meetings, conferences).
  • Intent: Genuine temporary stay; must leave when visa expires.
  • Funds: Enough money to support yourself (and family) and to leave New Zealand.
  • Onward travel: Proof of travel to leave New Zealand (e.g. return or onward ticket).
  • Character & health: Must meet character and health requirements; medical or police certificates may be required.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (valid for at least 3 months beyond intended departure from NZ).
  • Completed application and supporting documents as per Immigration NZ checklist.
  • Passport-size photograph meeting NZ photo specifications.
  • Proof of funds (bank statements, employment letter, sponsorship).
  • Evidence of ties to home country (employment, property, family).
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation; return or onward ticket.
  • If visiting family: invitation and proof of host’s status in New Zealand.

Application Process

  1. Check whether you need a visa or an NZeTA on the Immigration New Zealand website.
  2. Create an account online (Immigration NZ) and complete the Visitor visa application.
  3. Upload required documents and pay the application fee.
  4. Submit the application; you may be asked for more information or to provide biometrics.
  5. Wait for a decision; processing times vary by nationality and caseload.
  6. If approved, you will receive an e-visa or notification; ensure you carry proof when travelling.

Important Tips

  • Provide clear proof of funds and a credible travel plan.
  • Show strong ties to your home country to support genuine temporary intent.
  • Include return or onward travel; it is a standard requirement.
  • Apply early; do not confirm non-refundable travel before visa grant.
