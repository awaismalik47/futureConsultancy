United Kingdom

UK Standard Visitor Visa Requirements

Requirements for visiting the UK for tourism, business, or family. Apply with confidence using clear, professional guidance.

Home / Visit Visa / United Kingdom
Overview Eligibility Required Documents Application Process Processing & Fee Important Tips

Overview

The UK Standard Visitor visa is for short stays for tourism, business, study (short courses), or family visits. Most applicants apply online and then provide biometrics at a Visa Application Centre (VAC). You must show you will leave the UK at the end of your visit and have enough funds for your trip.

Eligibility

  • Purpose: Tourism, business, short study (e.g. up to 6 months), family visit, or medical treatment.
  • Intent: Genuine visitor; no intention to live or work in the UK.
  • Funds: Ability to support yourself and pay for return/onward travel.
  • Passport: Valid for the whole of your stay.
  • Ties: Strong links to your home country to show you will return.

Required Documents

  • Valid passport (with at least one blank page).
  • Completed online application and appointment confirmation.
  • Passport-size colour photograph per UKVI guidelines.
  • Proof of finances (bank statements, payslips, sponsorship letter).
  • Evidence of employment or studies (letter from employer/school).
  • Travel itinerary, accommodation, and purpose of visit.
  • If visiting family/friends: invitation letter and host’s status in the UK.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application on the official UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) website.
  2. Pay the visa fee and any Immigration Health Surcharge if applicable.
  3. Book and attend an appointment at a Visa Application Centre to submit documents and give biometrics.
  4. Your application is then sent to UKVI for decision; you may be asked for more documents.
  5. Processing usually takes around 3 weeks for standard applications; priority services may be available.
  6. If granted, collect your passport with the visa vignette or receive it by courier as per VAC process.

Important Tips

  • Submit a complete set of documents; missing evidence is a common reason for refusal.
  • Bank statements should show a clear, consistent history; avoid large unexplained deposits.
  • Invitation letters should include host’s full details and your relationship.
  • Apply in good time before your travel date; avoid last-minute applications.
